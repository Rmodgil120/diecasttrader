About Us

This website is for fellow die-cast collectors who wants to sell their own Die-cast models but don't know where to display those. My vision is simple, no nonsense website, just to display your cars here, along with prices and your contact information. The buyer shall contact you via your Email ID, then you people can share the delivery and contact information in private. Diecasttrader is not responsible for the delivery.

FAQs

Charges for displaying the model?

Price for listing is ₹5. The paywall of ₹5 will make sure that the seller is serious about the product and not just a troll. If the article is sold, I will not be taking any commission from the sale.

How does delivery work?

Once you contact the seller, by clicking on the Email / WhatsApp icon below the listing, mention to the seller that you are interested in buying their listed model. Once the seller verifies, they shall add the delivery cost to the listing price and inform the buyer the total amount. Models are sold by individuals here, and delivery is to be done by seller only, via India Post, DTDC etc.

Why is there no cart?

Each model is listed by a different person, and needs to be delivered individually by them only. Having a cart is not possible as you have to contact each and every seller personally to finalize the price of the article and delivery rates. I am here to just provide you with a platform where you can share your models that you want to sell.
